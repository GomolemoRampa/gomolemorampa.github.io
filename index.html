Things I've built

01 Full-Stack Platform

Stokvel Management Assistant

Stokvels are a cornerstone of South African financial culture — but most still run on WhatsApp messages and handwritten notebooks. We built the digital infrastructure they deserve: a full platform where groups track contributions, schedule payouts, and hold each other accountable with a clear audit trail.

3-tier architecture: React on the front, Node.js/Express in the middle, MongoDB at the back. Role-based access so admins and members see exactly what they need — and nothing they shouldn't.

03 Live Challenge ● Active

Twin Transition Challenge — TUHF21

The biggest barrier to green infrastructure in affordable rental housing isn't awareness — it's financing. Small-scale developers and resident landlords can't access the capital needed to implement solar water heaters, energy-efficient lighting, or water efficiency systems.

Working toward a solution that creates measurable, quantifiable efficiency gains in energy, water, and waste across TUHF21's portfolio of affordable housing units.
